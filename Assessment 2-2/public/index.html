About Novu

Novu—short for Narratives of You—is a space where Mise (The Author's Pen Name) shares her stories with the world. Dive into her tales, explore her imagination, and leave your thoughts in the comments.

Novu is all about connecting readers with the author's narratives, offering a place to enjoy, reflect, and engage with stories created just for you.
